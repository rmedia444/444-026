Cybersecurity Concerns

In 2010, cybersecurity was becoming an increasingly critical issue as the digital landscape expanded. High-profile data breaches and cyber-attacks highlighted vulnerabilities in both personal and corporate systems. The growing reliance on digital infrastructure for communication, commerce, and critical services underscored the need for robust cybersecurity measures to protect sensitive information and ensure the integrity of digital operations.
